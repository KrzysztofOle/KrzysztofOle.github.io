01 / ABOUT

An engineer with a practical approach to technology.

I graduated from the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering at Gdańsk University of Technology, where I studied Machine Design with a specialization in Robotics.

For most of my career, I have worked with industrial robots, automation integration and machine vision applications. I have also developed tools supporting the calibration of robot equipment and industrial cameras.

Today, software development and the practical use of artificial intelligence play an increasingly important role in my work. I am most interested in technology that delivers measurable improvements.

My wife runs the SOTAX accounting office. This gives me direct insight into the repetitive processes and everyday challenges faced by accountants and their clients.
